Privacy Policy for Weekly

Effective Date: 14th March 2026

Weekly respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

Information Collection and Use

Local Data Storage

  • The app does not require you to sign in or create an account.
  • Your habit and progress data is stored locally on your device in a local database.
  • This local data includes your habits, completion status, and progress tracking.

Analytics Data Collection

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our app and improve the user experience. This service automatically collects:

  • App Usage Data: Screen views, app opens, session duration
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version
  • Anonymous Identifiers: Randomly generated user IDs (not linked to personal identity)
  • General Location: Country/region (not precise location)

How We Use Information

  • Local Data: Used solely to provide app functionality and track your personal habits
  • Analytics Data: Used to improve app performance, understand user behavior, and fix bugs
  • No Personal Identification: We cannot identify you personally from the analytics data

Data Sharing

  • Local Data: Never shared with anyone - stays on your device
  • Analytics Data: Shared only with Google Firebase Analytics for processing
  • No Third-Party Sales: We do not sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

  • Google Firebase Analytics: For app usage analytics and performance monitoring
  • Firebase Analytics is governed by Google's Privacy Policy

Your Data Rights

  • Local Data: You can delete all local data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data
  • Analytics Opt-Out: You can disable analytics tracking through your device's advertising settings
  • Data Deletion: Analytics data is automatically deleted by Google after 26 months

Data Security

  • Local data is stored securely on your device using Android's built-in security features
  • Analytics data is transmitted securely to Google's servers using encryption
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Effective Date" and may provide additional notice within the app.
